What is SBC?

                   

Sniper Business Center (SBC), is a system designed to increase the visibility
                    of its members on WhatsApp and Telegram. Today, we have more than 40,000 members,
                      part of our database, knowing that the latter continues to
                      grow every day.
The Sniper Business Center currently operates with a web application,
                     which hosts each of its services and each of its activities. The Sniper
                       Business Center (SBC) has existed since September 2023, but acquired the legal status
                       of an establishment, recognized by the Cameroonian State on June 1, 2024.
The SBC offers its community growth opportunities for all those
who seek to expand their address book, for better visibility
of their offers and services, or for those who just want to
make new connections. Click on the "download document" button
to download the legal documents of the SBC

                   

Who is SBC for 📢?

                   

📱Are you looking to increase your business's visibility on Whatsapp or Telegram?

                       

SBC provides you with a database of over 30,000 contacts that increase
                    every day and will allow you to boost your visibility on both WhatsApp and Telegram.
                    This database is for the most part made up of Cameroonian contacts, but it also contains
                    contacts from other African countries and soon from the rest of the world

                   

🛒Discover the marketplace

                       

Still with the aim of increasing the visibility of members, as well as the products and services
                    they offer, the SBC provides its members with a marketplace. This is a
                    showcase that allows members to exhibit their products or services, so that other members who
                    need them can easily find them.

Withdrawal experience:
                     Withdrawals are fast, the minimum amount is 2000 FCFA and the withdrawal fees are 7%.

                       

Eligible countries:
                     The countries currently eligible to join SBC are: Cameroon, Togo, Benin, Burkina Faso,
Senegal, Congo Brazzaville, Cote D'ivoire.

                       

Registration and subscription:
                     Registration is free and there are two types of subscription: standard which is 2150 FCFA and
                     Premium which is 5000 FCFA. The difference with the premium subscription is the advanced targeting of contacts
                     (download targeted contacts by: country, city, age, gender, interests). Otherwise, the other offers
                      do not change with the standard subscription.

                     

How does payment work?:

What happens after my registration and my subscription?:
